### 🚨 Load-bearing finding — v01 §4 hypothesis was WRONG

v01 §4 hypothesised: *"casual loading STACKS on top of penalty rates per Modern Award convention"*. The verbatim FWC text (cl 11.3(b)+(c) + cl 19.5) says the **OPPOSITE**:

- Casual 25% loading paid only on ordinary Mon-Fri hours.
- On Sat/Sun/PH ordinary: penalty multiplier on base rate ALONE — no casual stacking.
- On overtime: casual loading NOT paid.

Calc engine consequence: penalty / OT multipliers apply to the bare minimum hourly rate for casuals, never to (rate × 1.25). Apete is full-time so cl 11.3 doesn't directly affect him, but Phase 1+ casual workers (esp. MA000009 hospitality Mia) need this rule from day 1. Encoded prominently in v02 §11.3 with a correction callout.
