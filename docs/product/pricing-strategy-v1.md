# Product — pricing strategy v1

## Two tiers

### Free — PALM
- **Audience:** PALM scheme visa workers (Pacific Australia Labour Mobility).
- **Price:** $0 forever. Not a trial. Not a discount. Free.
- **Why:** Power asymmetry is highest for PALM workers; charging them for the right to check their pay is misaligned with the product's purpose.
- **Eligibility:** Self-declared visa class at signup. We do not verify (trust + safety-first).
- **Limits:** Same feature set as paid; no artificial gating.

### Paid — Australian award-covered ($4.99/mo, AUD)
- **Audience:** Australian workers on Modern Awards. Launching with hospitality (Mia persona).
- **Price:** $4.99/mo or $49.99/yr (~17% annual discount).
- **What it unlocks:** Same product as PALM tier, just a paid-for usage relationship for non-PALM users.
- **Why $4.99:** Coffee-money price; meaningful enough to reduce churn on signup; cheap enough that the household-share question is "yeah just sign up" not "we should split this".

## Why two tiers (not paywall vs free trial)

The point isn't gating features. PALM workers and Aussie casuals get the same product. The price split is a values statement: most-vulnerable get this for free, others contribute.

## What's NEVER paywalled (regardless of tier)
- Confirming facts.
- Running a comparison.
- Exporting your data.
- Deleting your data.

If a feature is part of the basic information-tool function, it is not behind a paywall in either tier. The product is the product.

## Phase rollout
- **Phase 0–1:** Free for everyone (Apete + household).
- **Phase 2:** Paid tier launches. PALM tier remains free. New non-PALM signups go to paid.
- **Phase 3+:** No price change anticipated.

## Future considerations (parked, not committed)
- Sliding scale for non-PALM low-income workers.
- Employer / union / NGO bulk licences (for distribution, not gating).
- One-time export-only fee for PDF reports.

These are NOT decisions. They are park-list items for a later pricing review.

## Why this exists
Pricing is positioning. A coffee-priced subscription with a free PALM tier signals what we believe about who deserves access. Document it so future-Jovi doesn't drift.
