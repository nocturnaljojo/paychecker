## Process
- Use `.claude/agents/researcher.md` to produce a draft.
- Use `.claude/skills/SKILL-AWARD-add-new.md` to walk it into reference data.
- Update `.claude/ref/REF-AWARDS-list.md` when status changes.

## Cadence
- New award: research note before any reference data is encoded.
- Existing award: re-research between 1 June and 1 July annually (FWC variation cycle).
- Variation orders out of cycle: re-research within 7 days of becoming aware.

## Why this file exists
The list of award notes deserves its own index — opening `docs/research/` and listing files works for 4 entries but rots once we hit 10–15 awards.
